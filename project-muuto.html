Brand Identity & UX/UI Redesign Preview

Muuto Website Redesign

A redesign concept for Muuto focused on strengthening brand recognition, improving the digital experience, and creating a more cohesive visual system while preserving the brand's Scandinavian heritage.

About the Brand

Muuto Heritage

Muuto is a contemporary furniture and interior design brand rooted in Scandinavian tradition. Its core mission is to bring new perspectives to Nordic design through simplicity, craftsmanship, and constant innovation.

The Challenge

New Perspectives

While Muuto enjoys a strong visual presence, its current digital branding lacks distinctiveness and does not fully express the concept behind its name, which is derived from the Finnish word muutos ("new perspective").

The core challenge was to construct a more recognizable visual identity while maintaining the premium, ultra-minimalist character of the brand.

New Logo Concept

Modular Brand Mark

The redesigned identity introduces a modular symbol built entirely from the letter "M". The mark reflects one of Muuto's core values: the ability to combine, customize, and build products in flexible ways. Inspired by shelving systems, the symbol functions both as an initial and a standalone asset.
