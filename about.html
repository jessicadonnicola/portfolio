About Me

Hi!

I’m a Junior UX/UI Designer with a background in development and a deep curiosity for everything that makes digital experiences simpler, clearer, and more human.

I’m currently completing a Master’s in UX/UI Design at start2impact University, where I’m expanding my skills in User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Accessibility (WCAG), and UI Design.

Before moving into design, I worked as a Salesforce Developer — an experience that taught me how to collaborate with different stakeholders, navigate technical constraints, and create solutions that work in real-world, complex environments.

Outside the digital world, I create handcrafted jewelry using soft soldering techniques in a small workshop container in the countryside. It’s a slow, hands-on process — the complete opposite of digital design, and perhaps that’s exactly why I love it.
